About The Business

For A Loop is a business founded by Alexandra Lucas. Every item is hand crafted and made with the highest quality. From whales to chinchillas, and hedgehogs to pumpkins, you can rest assured that you will always have the highest quality craftsmanship there is! What started as a simple hobby has become so much more!
