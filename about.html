Who Is Alexandra?

Alexandra Lucas is the creator of For A Loop. What started out as a simple hobby for her turned into a business that she enjoys. She also loves to create art and has even illustrated a children's book that is published on Amazon. The title of the book is Dino Dad's Journey: A Dinosaur Adventure! She is currently attending college at Arizona State University and strives to become an elementary school teacher.

Activities

  • Alexandra loves doing art. Between drawing and painting, it is one of her favorite hobbies! She even paints magnets to hang up on the fridge!

  • Alexandra loves to go on hikes and be outside whenever she gets the chance. She will go hiking every so often and loves to go see the scenery and take pictures!

  • Alexandra loves gardening and taking care of her plants. Some of her favorites are her succulents that do very well here in Arizona! She loves to name them all!
